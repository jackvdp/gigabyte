What we've done

Client: JM2 Group

We built a supplier database and automated communication for JM2 Group, enhancing their efficiency in managing subcontractors. This allowed them to scale from handling 10 jobs a day to 100 jobs without additional administrative staff, significantly improving their operations and setting the stage for future growth.

Client: Consult Construct

We replaced Consult Construct’s outdated document management system with a custom no-code intranet and workflow management system. This streamlined their document consistency and workflow efficiency, enabling fast access to templates, reducing lost documents, and enhancing overall productivity.

Client: Virgin Land

We implemented a phased plan using off-the-shelf tools and later custom-built systems to automate Virgin Land’s land acquisition process. This agile approach allowed them to secure angel investment and eventually get acquired, preparing them for Series A funding and future innovations in machine learning.

Client: Frankham Group

We utilized AI to extract and enrich contact data from email systems for Frankham Group, integrating this data into their new CRM system. This added over £2 million to their sales pipeline, demonstrating the transformative potential of AI in enhancing client relationships and driving business growth.
