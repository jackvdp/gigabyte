How our process works

Discovery

Your tech strategy workshop

Everything starts with a discovery session where we deep-dive into:

  • What your technology needs to do.
  • What your technology isn't currently doing.
  • What systems and processes you have in place.

We'll also work with you to find out where your business is going, to make sure your tech is future-proofed.

This part of the process is essential. By doing this, it means you're not making a decision on your tech that you regret a few years down the line. We're making sure the tech upgrades and changes you make now serve you long-term.

Recommendations

After the discovery session, we'll know enough about your business, customers, and suppliers to make our recommendation. This recommendation will be to either:

  1. Getting the best from what you already have.
  2. Help you buy an off-the-shelf product.
  3. Integrate any software needed to fill gaps.
  4. Build you a fully bespoke system if nothing currently exists to suit your needs.

We'll explain exactly why and how we came to the conclusion. This consultancy-led approach means you're confident in the decision you make and fully understand why we're doing what we're doing.

Solutions

Our approach isn't one-size-fits-all; we offer a range of options designed to best serve your unique business needs and goals.

Based on our findings, we'll recommend one of these four strategies:

  1. Getting the most from your existing software
  2. Buying off-the-shelf solutions
  3. Integration of new and existing systems
  4. Developing fully bespoke solutions
Let's explore each of these options below.

Our solutions

We may discover you do not need any new systems and in fact, with some small tweaks, can make your existing systems work better for you. We’ll show you how to make these tweaks (such as automating processes) without the need to introduce any new technology.

In other cases, we can work with your existing team to streamline your processes.

We recommend this option when we think you can get more from what you’re currently using.

If your current tech isn’t up to scratch, the next step is to research off-the-shelf solutions available to see if any suit your needs. We quickly identify what’s available on the market and match you up with the best piece of software (if one exists). Since we’re 100% independent, you’re getting an honest expert opinion. And since we’re property industry experts, we know all your best AI and software options.

We’ll put together a summary to help you understand the trade-offs you may have to make that when buying software off the shelf. It could that one is really expensive, but does everything you need. Or another is cheaper, but new and so more unstable. We’ll present all your options to you and be clear about the benefits and drawbacks of each.

Most off-the-shelf products do 80% of what your business needs! Which is good news. If the remaining 20% is essential for your business, we can build add-on software to fill the gaps. This is a much more cost-effective solution than building completely from scratch: something that isn’t always needed.

We’ll map out your existing in-house system, which could be your CRM, and the data inside it to help us see how they relate to each other. After this, we’ll make sure these systems are working seamlessly together, building a small piece of software to do that (if there’s nothing off-the-shelf available).

We love building new bespoke systems and apps - if that’s what you need! If no off-the-shelf product or potential integration exists, we will recommend building from scratch.

Depending on the size, we may handle the build ourselves. Or if it’s too big or it works out to be more cost-effective, we’ll advise that you outsource the build to an offshore partner.

To avoid any management or communication challenges that may crop up, there’s the option for us to act as a conduit between you and the offshore developers to ensure they understand what they're building (to avoid confusion and costs spiralling).
