Why the property industry?

Building a suite of software for one of the UK’s largest asset management agencies opened our eyes to the difficulties you face with your tech.

Once we dug deeper, we discovered your challenges are made worse by being mis-sold software that’s not right for you. It’s why the property industry doesn’t need another software development house. You need independent technology consultants dedicated to finding the right systems for your business that save you time, money, and resources.

For the past 20 years, we’ve helped all kinds of businesses with this approach, such as: property maintenance teams, risk management businesses, land agents and developers.

Our values

Property industry experts

We speak your language. Speak to us like colleagues – we get it. Our 20+ years in the industry means understand the terminology, nuances, and context of property - including the challenges you, suppliers to the property industry, face.

We understand how everything works together as a system, and so we know how to find (or build) the right tech you need.

100% Independent

An unbiassed expert opinion. Typically, when you’re searching for new technology, you have to sit on demo after demo with people from different software providers. They exaggerate the features of their product and don’t mention its limitations.

They’re not matching you with the right software; they’re trying to sell you their product. With our access to the full market, we’re 100% independent and 100% focussed on you.

Consultancy led

We find the right solution. Our unique consultancy-first approach means we’ll work with you to find the right tech to solve the right problem. This means mapping out your current processes and systems to find out what they’re not currently doing.

We’ll also do deep discovery work to understand your goals and shape our recommendations around this - to match you with the best and most cost-effective solution.
